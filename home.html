For most of 2019, I took part in the quantified self movement. I tracked my steps, recorded runs, downloaded listening history. And surprisingly, all of this was fairly passive.


Tech companies collect huge amounts of data from users, so downloading my history was trivial. Inspired by the Feltron reports, I took the month of January 2020 to reflect on last year through visualizing this data.


All posters are designed and coded entirely using Javascript and the p5.js library.
